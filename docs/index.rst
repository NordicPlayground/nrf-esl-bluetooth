With Bluetooth 5.4 (PAwR and EAD) and the introduction of Electronic Shelf Label (ESL) Profile/Service, Bluetooth SIG is standardizing Electronic Shelf Label use case, accelerating the digital transformation in retail stores.

In this documentation, you will find information about the `Nordic Semiconductor website`_ implementation of the Bluetooth Low Energy Electronic Shelf Label (ESL) Profile, and the instructions on how to use ESL Central (Access Point - AP) and ESL Peripheral (Tag) samples `nrf-esl-bluetooth`_ in conjunction with `nRF Connect SDK 2.5.0`_.
