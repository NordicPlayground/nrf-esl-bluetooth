Welcome to nRF Connect SDK – ESL Profile and Service V2.4.0

With Bluetooth 5.4 (PAwR and EAD) and the introduction of Electronic Shelf Label (ESL) Profile/Service, Bluetooth SIG is standardizing Electronic Shelf Label use case, accelerating the digital transformation in retail stores.

In this documentation, you will find information about the Nordic Semiconductor website implementation of the Bluetooth Low Energy Electronic Shelf Label (ESL) Profile, and the instructions on how to use ESL Central (Access Point - AP) and ESL Peripheral (Tag) samples nrf-esl-bluetooth in conjunction with nRF Connect SDK 2.4.0.
